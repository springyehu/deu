Admissions at DEU

Thank you for your interest in Digital European University! We welcome talented and ambitious students from across Europe and around the world to join our vibrant academic community.

How to Apply

  1. Explore Programs: Review our academic programs to find the best fit for your interests and career goals.
  2. Check Requirements: Verify the specific admission requirements for your chosen program, including academic prerequisites and language proficiency (most programs are taught in English; TOEFL iBT 90+ or IELTS 6.5+ generally required for non-native speakers).
  3. Prepare Documents: Gather necessary documents:
    • Official academic transcripts and diplomas (certified English translations if not originally in English or German).
    • Proof of English language proficiency.
    • Curriculum Vitae (CV) / Resume.
    • Letter of Motivation / Statement of Purpose.
    • Letters of Recommendation (typically 2, for Master's and PhD).
    • Copy of passport/ID.
    • Portfolio (for certain creative programs).
  4. Online Application: Complete and submit your application through our online portal by the specified deadline.
  5. Admission Decision: You will be notified of the admission decision via email. Successful applicants will receive an offer letter and further instructions.

Application Deadlines

  • For Fall Semester (starts October):
    • Non-EU/EEA applicants: May 31st
    • EU/EEA applicants: July 15th
  • For Spring Semester (starts April, selected programs):
    • Non-EU/EEA applicants: November 30th
    • EU/EEA applicants: January 15th

Please note: These are general deadlines. Some programs may have different timelines. Always check the specific program page.

Tuition Fees & Scholarships

As a modern European university, DEU aims to provide accessible high-quality education. Tuition fees vary by program and applicant origin (EU/EEA vs. Non-EU/EEA).

Typical annual tuition for Bachelor's programs: €8,000 (EU/EEA), €12,000 (Non-EU/EEA).

Typical annual tuition for Master's programs: €10,000 (EU/EEA), €15,000 (Non-EU/EEA).

DEU offers a range of merit-based and need-based scholarships for outstanding international and domestic students. Please visit our "Scholarships & Financial Aid" section (hypothetical page) for more details and application procedures.
