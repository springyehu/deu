About Digital European University (DEU)

Our History

Digital European University (DEU) was established in 2005 as a forward-thinking institution dedicated to addressing the evolving needs of the 21st century. Founded through a unique partnership between leading technology firms and academic pioneers, DEU was envisioned as a hub for innovation, digital literacy, and European collaboration. Located in the vibrant city of Berlin, Germany, we embrace a modern approach to higher education.

From our inception, DEU has been committed to interdisciplinary studies, practical application of knowledge, and fostering a global perspective among our students.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To deliver transformative education and pioneering research that equip individuals to thrive in a digitally interconnected world, fostering innovation, critical thinking, and ethical leadership with a strong European and global consciousness.

Vision: To be a leading European university renowned for its excellence in digital-age education, impactful research, and its contribution to a sustainable and inclusive global society.

Core Values

  • Innovation: Championing creativity and the pursuit of new knowledge and solutions.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in teaching, research, and service.
  • Collaboration: Fostering partnerships within and beyond the university.
  • Integrity: Upholding ethical principles in all endeavors.
  • Inclusivity: Creating a diverse, equitable, and welcoming environment for all.
  • Global Citizenship: Encouraging responsibility towards European and global communities.

Campus Life

Our state-of-the-art campus in Berlin offers a dynamic environment for learning and growth. Facilities include advanced digital labs, collaborative workspaces, a modern library with extensive digital resources, and vibrant student life centers. We encourage participation in various clubs, societies, and international exchange programs.
