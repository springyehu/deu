Academic Programs

Digital European University offers a range of innovative Bachelor's, Master's, and PhD programs designed to prepare students for successful careers in the global digital economy. Our curriculum is industry-relevant and research-informed.

Schools & Faculties

School of Computing & Digital Technologies

Focuses on the core principles and applications of computer science, artificial intelligence, and data.

  • B.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • B.Sc. in Cybersecurity & Network Engineering
  • B.Sc. in Data Science & Analytics
  • M.Sc. in Advanced Software Engineering
  • M.Sc. in Cloud Computing & DevOps
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science

School of Business & Innovation Management

Prepares leaders for the dynamic world of digital business, entrepreneurship, and sustainable innovation.

  • B.A. in Digital Business & E-Commerce
  • B.A. in Fintech & Financial Innovation
  • B.A. in Sustainable Management & Entrepreneurship
  • M.Sc. in Digital Marketing & Analytics
  • MBA in Technology Management
  • Ph.D. in Innovation Studies

School of European Studies & Digital Humanities

Explores European culture, policy, and society through the lens of digital transformation and global interconnectedness.

  • B.A. in European Digital Policy & Governance
  • B.A. in Digital Culture & Media Studies
  • M.A. in European Integration & Global Affairs
  • M.A. in Digital Humanities & Cultural Heritage
  • Ph.D. in European Studies

School of Smart Environments & Sustainability

Addresses critical challenges in creating intelligent and sustainable urban and natural environments.

  • B.Eng. in Smart City Technologies
  • B.Sc. in Renewable Energy Systems
  • M.Sc. in Environmental Informatics & Modelling
  • M.Eng. in Sustainable Infrastructure Engineering
  • Ph.D. in Sustainability Science

Continuing Education & Professional Development

DEU also offers a variety of short courses, certificates, and executive programs for professionals looking to upskill in areas like Big Data, AI applications, Digital Transformation, and Green Technologies.

For detailed course descriptions and program structures, please visit the respective school pages or contact our admissions office.
